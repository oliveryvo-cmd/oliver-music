Who is Oliver?
OLIVER
Oliver is a 10-year-old pop artist, songwriter, and world-builder from California.

He has Tourette syndrome. He built a social network, a government, a country, and a platform for every disabled kid on earth — all before age 11.

Now he's making music.

His debut single "I Feel You" is about the moment he told a friend with a disability: "I understand — because I have one too."

Pop. Inspiring. Real.
